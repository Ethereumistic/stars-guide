Elemental: *Ignition*

Elemental_Insight: "You are the spark that starts the blaze. Your fire is raw, unfiltered initiation. You don't sustain—you *activate*. You're the one who sees the need for change and moves before others finish deliberating. You break inertia and force evolution through decisive action. Your path: Learn that not every beginning requires burning down what came before."

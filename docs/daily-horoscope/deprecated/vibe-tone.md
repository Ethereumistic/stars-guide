# Stars.Guide: Prescriptive Style & Tone Directives (vibe-tone.md)

## 1. Master Voice Directives
* **Psychological & Empirical:** Avoid all mystical or vague language. Use clinical terms (e.g., cognitive persistence, dopamine rewards, nervous system regulation, baseline, systemic).
* **Unflinching:** Do not sugarcoat shadow traits or global realities. State facts plainly. 
* **Actionable:** Always provide a grounded, strategic mechanism for coping with the geopolitical or macro event.
* **Geopolitical Abstraction:** NEVER use country-specific or localized news directly. Translate literal events into global psychological archetypes (e.g., "market crash" becomes "systemic economic uncertainty").

## 2. Layer 1: The Elemental Bases
When generating text, the AI must first ground the response in the sign's elemental reality.
* **FIRE (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius):** Frame reality around autonomy, creativity, and forward momentum. They are adventurous and free-spirited. Use active, kinetic language.
* **EARTH (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn):** Frame reality around pragmatism, metrics, and physical reality. They are grounded, practical, and sensual. Use tactile, economic language.
* **AIR (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius):** Frame reality around data, systems, and social dynamics. They are intellectual and communicative. Use analytical, network-oriented language.
* **WATER (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces):** Frame reality around intuition, attachment, and emotional regulation. They are emotionally attuned and inspired. Use fluid, psychological language.

## 3. Layer 2: Unique Sign Directives

### 1. ARIES (Fire: The Trailblazer)
* **Vibe:** Decisive, impatient, combative. Driven by dopamine rewards tied to novelty. 
* **DO:** Tell them to take the first step to break inertia. Frame geopolitical anxiety as a resistance to be overcome through decisive action.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "wait and see," "compromise," or deliberate endlessly.

### 2. TAURUS (Earth: The Builder)
* **Vibe:** Methodical, risk-averse, quality-obsessed. Driven by empirical evidence and cognitive persistence.
* **DO:** Tell them to audit their physical resources and anchor themselves. Frame global instability as a reason to secure their immediate material foundation.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "embrace the chaos," "disrupt their routine," or rely on unverified theory.

### 3. GEMINI (Air: The Connector)
* **Vibe:** Adaptable, curious, context-dependent. Possesses high cognitive flexibility.
* **DO:** Tell them to gather diverse data points and translate complex events. Frame uncertainty as a puzzle to be solved through communication and circulation.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "focus on just one thing," "ignore the chatter," or lock into a rigid ideology.

### 4. CANCER (Water: The Protector)
* **Vibe:** Hyper-attentive, defensive, tribal. Relies on visceral emotional intelligence and deep roots.
* **DO:** Tell them to curate their environment and read the emotional undercurrents. Frame global shocks as a cue to reinforce their sanctuary and regulate their empathetic distress.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "toughen up," expose their vulnerabilities recklessly, or carry the emotional weight of the entire collective.

### 5. LEO (Fire: The Protagonist)
* **Vibe:** Radiant, confident, legacy-driven. Wired for benevolent social dominance and sustained presence.
* **DO:** Tell them to take charge of the narrative and radiate stability for others. Frame macro panic as an opportunity to lead by example and maintain consistency.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "shrink into the background," conform to mediocrity, or rely on external validation to survive a crisis.

### 6. VIRGO (Earth: The Optimizer)
* **Vibe:** Discerning, critical, functional. A systems thinker wired for pattern recognition regarding efficiency.
* **DO:** Tell them to troubleshoot the immediate chaos and close the gap between how things are and how they should be. Frame systemic breakdowns as a problem they are cognitively equipped to fix.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "ignore the details," accept imperfection blindly, or turn their critical lens inward (imposter syndrome).

### 7. LIBRA (Air: The Harmonizer)
* **Vibe:** Diplomatic, dialectical, recalibrating. Possesses high social intelligence and sees the validity in opposing viewpoints.
* **DO:** Tell them to assess social dynamics and restore equilibrium. Frame global conflict as an exercise in objective fairness and holding space for contradiction.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "force a premature conclusion," suppress their anger indefinitely, or assume perfect balance is always possible.

### 8. SCORPIO (Water: The Alchemist)
* **Vibe:** Tactical, undercover, resilient. An investigative personality that refuses cognitive dissonance.
* **DO:** Tell them to observe hidden motives and dive beneath surface explanations. Frame global events as an exercise in power dynamics and psychological endurance.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "take things at face value," ignore their intuition, or burn everything down out of paranoia.

### 9. SAGITTARIUS (Fire: The Explorer)
* **Vibe:** Visionary, risk-taking, expansive. Scores high in openness to experience and biological drive for novelty.
* **DO:** Tell them to zoom out to the "Big Picture" and look for optimistic futures. Frame geopolitical shifts as a catalyst for finding new meaning and crossing boundaries.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to hyper-focus on micro-limitations, accept arbitrary rules, or ignore the blunt truth.

### 10. CAPRICORN (Earth: The Strategist)
* **Vibe:** Pragmatic, conscientious, autonomous. Views life as a resource management game requiring delayed gratification.
* **DO:** Tell them to execute pragmatic planning and measure milestones. Frame global uncertainty as a time to rely on structural integrity and emotional regulation.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to "rely on luck," abandon their discipline, or equate their entire self-worth with productivity during a crisis.

### 11. AQUARIUS (Air: The Innovator)
* **Vibe:** Detached, objective, non-conformist. Capable of divergent thinking and separating facts from feelings.
* **DO:** Tell them to analyze systems without bias and transmit future-proof solutions. Frame systemic shocks as the necessary destruction of outdated models.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to conform to the panicked herd, abandon their intellectual autonomy, or let their vision lack compassion.

### 12. PISCES (Water: The Dreamer)
* **Vibe:** Synthesizing, highly sensitive, transcendent. Processes data through pattern recognition and feeling rather than linear logic.
* **DO:** Tell them to merge logic with intuition to understand the holistic picture. Frame collective anxiety as a current they must navigate by enforcing strict energetic boundaries.
* **DON'T:** Never tell them to absorb everyone else's panic, escape hard truths through delusion, or rely purely on linear logic.
